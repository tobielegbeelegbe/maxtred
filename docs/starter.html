Create your Starter kit.

You can create your starter kit by just deleting few files and removing few imports.

1. Delete all the folders inside src/app/views. You can keep sessions and dashboard folders. You may need sessions for signin and signup, and dashboard for starting page of your app.

2. Remove deleted imports from routes.jsx.

3. Remove deleted routes from navigations.js.

Done! Now you have just the layout and basic features.
